Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?

Click "Register" button, fill your details (name, email, DOB, phone, password) and verify your account. Registration takes less than 2 minutes.

Is my vote secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption, secure servers, and follow international security standards. Your vote is completely encrypted and tamper-proof.

Can I vote multiple times?

No, our system prevents double voting. Once you vote, you cannot vote again. This is strictly enforced both in frontend and backend.

What if I want to vote for NOTA?

NOTA (None of the Above) option is available alongside all candidates. Your NOTA vote carries the same weight as any candidate vote.

When can I vote?

You can vote anytime during the election period as announced by the admin. The system displays exact start and end times.

Can I change my vote after submission?

No, once submitted, your vote is final and cannot be changed. This ensures election integrity and prevents vote manipulation.
